Why face recognition app is important ?

Facial recognition is a way of identifying or confirming an individual’s identity using their face. Facial recognition systems can be used to identify people in photos, videos, or in real-time.Facial recognition is a category of biometric security.

Unlocking phones

Few Android phonesuses an iPhone-like facial recognition feature to recognise a user’s face in order to unlock the phone.

Finding missing persons

The technology helped identify thousands of missing children within just four days of launching

Tracking student or worker attendance

It uses finger and face recognition system to verify persons identity and record its time-in and time-out with all required details.
